Values-In-Action


During my 4 years in ACS (Barker Road), I have participated in the school-based Values-In-Action (VIA). Examples of VIA include: "Hampers of Love", "Love my School Environment" and "MWS Senior Activity Centre Engagement".

No. VIA Descriptipn
1. Hampers of Love My class and I created hampers and gave them to the elderly during Chinese New Year.
2. Love my School Environment My class and I went around the school to help the workers and cleaners in their school with some of their jobs like sweeping the classrooms or mopping the floors.
3. MWS Senior Activity Centre Engagement My class and I had to organise and travel to an old folks home and spend time with the elderly there by playing games and interacting with them.
