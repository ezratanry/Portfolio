CCA


In Secondary 2, I joined Concert Band as my Co-Curricular Activity (CCA). During my year as a Secondary 3 student, I participated in performing for the school's Chinese New Year and National Day Celebration. I also represented the school at the Singapore International Band Festival (SIBF) and obtained the Silver Award. Along with attending the SIBF Workshop, I also took part in the Concert Band Exchange where many different schools all performed for each other. Additionally, I have also taken part in the Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) and has been awarded an Accomplishment.
